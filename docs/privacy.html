Privacy Policy

Ippo MVP — by Legacy of Education, Inc.

Introduction

Ippo MVP ("the App") is an effort-based running game for Apple Watch and iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

The App accesses the following health and fitness data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Heart rate — used to measure effort during runs
  • Distance — used to track run progress
  • Active energy burned — used for workout summaries
  • Workouts — used to log and validate running sessions

This data is only accessed with your explicit permission through the HealthKit authorization prompt.

How We Use Your Data

Your health and fitness data is used exclusively for:

  • Sprint validation and effort calculation during gameplay
  • Workout tracking and session history
  • Rank progression based on running effort

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose unrelated to core app functionality.

Data Storage

Your account data and gameplay progress are stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS) and stored in compliance with Google Cloud's security standards.

Health data read from HealthKit is processed on-device and only aggregated metrics (e.g., effort scores, distance totals) are stored server-side. Raw HealthKit data is not uploaded to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data or health data to any third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App by navigating to Settings → Delete Account. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
